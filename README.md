# Talk Report — PPG Digital IT Insights (19 November)

Author: Logapriyah  
Date: 19 November  
Course: Technology and Information System

## Summary
On 19th November, I attended a talk on PPG Digital IT Insights as part of our Technology and Information System course. The session explained how a large organization applies technologies such as data analytics, IT infrastructure, and SAP to support business operations. It provided real-world insight into how these systems improve efficiency and decision-making, and encouraged me to develop my digital skills for future career opportunities.  
#PPGDigitalITInsights

## Purpose
- Learn how enterprise IT (data analytics, infrastructure, ERP) supports business processes.
- Understand practical applications of technology in large organizations.
- Explore career-relevant skills and industry expectations.

## Highlights
- Overview of data analytics use cases that drive business decisions.
- Explanation of IT infrastructure components that enable scalability and reliability.
- Demonstration or discussion of SAP (ERP) capabilities for enterprise resource planning.
- Insights into how IT supports operational efficiency and strategic goals.

## Key Topics Observed
- Data analytics workflows and business intelligence applications
- Enterprise IT architecture and infrastructure considerations
- SAP/ERP usage for integrating business functions
- Alignment between technology solutions and organizational objectives

## What I Learned
- How analytics and ERP tools help organizations make more informed decisions.
- The role of robust IT infrastructure in supporting large-scale operations.
- Practical examples of technology solving business problems (efficiency, visibility, integration).
- Which digital skills are valuable for enterprise environments.

## Takeaways
- Enterprise technology is a combination of tools, processes, and people working together.
- Exposure to real-world IT strategies clarified the career pathways in data and systems roles.
- I’m motivated to build skills in analytics, cloud/infrastructure fundamentals, and enterprise software.

## Next Steps (Personal)
- Study foundational SAP/ERP concepts and basic workflows.
- Practice data analytics (SQL, visualization tools) with sample business datasets.
- Learn about cloud and infrastructure basics (networking, server management).
- Seek internships or projects that expose me to enterprise-scale systems.

## Acknowledgments
Thank you to the speakers from PPG Digital and the course instructors for the informative session and practical insighT.
